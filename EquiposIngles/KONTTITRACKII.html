Description:

Our solution for monitoring, tracking and monitoring of cargo containers is KonttiTrack II in an integrated and developed in Colombia system incorporating a lock or electronic seal high security , with patented design, highly resistant to shock, vandalism and conditions extreme environment , equipped with the latest technology GPS tracking and data transmission GSM / GPRS. When it installed on the doors of a cargo container to detect , through their strategically placed sensors,handling, opening it and without requiring movement human intervention , besides the effective tracking and traceability of the load and not just the vehicle the transports . The main objective of KonttiTrack II is the monitoring, control and traceability of container load throughout the entire logistics supply chain, without interfering in the operations of loading and unloading, transport, storage and inspections by the authorities; as it can easily installed and uninstalled in less than 10 seconds by anyone not requiring the use of keys or special attachments without opening the container at any time. In addition , we have our own development platform, software tools and systems geographical information, especially designed exclusively for control, monitoring, tracking and analysis information on supply chain cargo containers; which run under a robust technology infrastructure, equipment , servers and communication channels; maintained , updated and supported directly by us.

DVR 3.0

AVL

Description:

The DVR 3.0 series mobile digital video recorder is a compact, full-featured recording system that uses a Hard Drive as a storage device. The recorder unit and associated accessories are specically designed for operation in a mobile environment. The DVR 3.0 system, used in conjunction with the cameras, records up to four channels of full-motion video and audio data to a laptop type Hard Drive (2.5” SATA). The rmware-driven menu system provides a simple method for conguring the unit's operation as well as searching for and viewing previously recorded AV records.

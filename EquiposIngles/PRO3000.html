PRO 3000

AVL

Description:

The PRO 3000 is an economical, full-featured vehicle tracking product designed for easy and reliable installation in automobiles. Is an ideal solution for automotive insurance, driver behavior management, auto rental and automotive applications when access to the vehicle diagnostics interface (OBD-II) is required.

Competitive everything

The PRO 3000 full featured tracking unit from Prossea features a small size, superior GPS design, OBD-II interface, and a 3-axis accelerometer. These features enable the PRO 3000 to access vehicle diagnostic interface data, track vehicle speed and location, plus detect hard braking, cornering or acceleration. Superior internal antennas for both cellular and GPS eliminate the need for professional installation and make the PRO 3000 install quick, easy and inexpensive. Messages are transported across the cellular network using enhanced SMS or UDP messaging providing a reliable communications link between the device and your application servers. The PRO 3000 is designed to dramatically reduce cost, power and size while signicantly improving eld reliablity in 12 volt passenger or light-duty vehicles.

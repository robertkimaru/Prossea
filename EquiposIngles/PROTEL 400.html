PROTEL 400

Personal

Description:

is a life tracking device with built-in GPS/GLONASS and GSM connectivity, which is able to collect device coordinates and other useful data to transfer them via GSM network to server. Device can be used as a mobile phone for call centers or in applications where location acquirement of remote objects is needed.

Alarm button Sleep operation to save energy

It can be used without GSM connection and out of online tracking Includes micro USB Cable , Strap, mini screwdriver , Charger 12V to 5V, Case
Specifications
Internal antennas GSM/GPS/GLONASS
Weight 80g
GPS/GLONASS Compatible protocols INMEA, GGA, GGL, GSA, GSV, RMC, VTG, GPRS class 10
Quad-band 900/1800 Mhz, 850/1900 Mhz
Sending Data GPRS (protocolos TCP/IP y UDP/IP)
Sensitivity -165 dBm
Temperature 0° a 50° C
Internal battery 1050mAh (Li-ion)
